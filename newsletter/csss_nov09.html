Git Er' Done on Github

Tuesday November 10th 5:00PM - TASC9204 West

Git is absolutely vital for jobs and the future for collaborative development in our industry. Microsoft is partnering with the CSSS to give you the best crash course in Git available. Understand the basics of Git, Github, and add something NEW to your resume. Additionally get a chance to meet and get to know more people in your beloved faculty of applied science. Free pop & pizza will be available.

We will go over using git on terminal for Linux/Unix as well as git with Visual Studio Community 2015 and Github.

Organized by your Microsoft Student Partner & the CSSS


SFU WICS & WEG Hackathon

Saturday November 14th 8:30AM - TASC9204

Do you enjoy coding?

Do you have friends you would like to code with OR compete against?

If your answer is YES, we have a FREE event that's just right for you and your friends!

Come and get totally absorbed in code for a whole day at the International Women's Hackathon, hosted by WEG and WiCS, made possible by a generous sponsorship from Mobify and contributions from PMC-Sierra and EA.

VENUE: TASC 9204, SFU Burnaby Campus
TIME: 08:30- 21:30
THEME : Accessibility: create or modify a mobile/web app to better serve those with disabilities or special needs

Participation is FREE and open to ANYONE (all genders welcome!) in Computer Science or Engineering Science who supports females in tech!

You will have 12 hours to work in teams of up to 4 to build a creative and innovative phone/web application with support from our Hackathon mentors. You'll then have a chance to pitch your project to a panel of judges for the chance to win GREAT PRIZES!

DON'T FORGET TO BRING: Your laptop, power cord (+ any other necessary development devices) and your creativity!

To register, please follow the link below: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1TVEjYJr0uqhaNRrmlYOpxdYg88sb3igx6UZOlvysBis/viewform?usp=send_form
The deadline to register is Nov 13th! Limited spots only so register soon! Hope to see you all there!

CSSS Hack Time

Wednesday November 18th 3:30PM - ASB9896

**HackTime cancelled on Wednesday November 11th due to Remembrance Day.**

HackTime is essentially time that the CSSS has book marked out for students to come and just work on any project they desire. That may be a group designing a game, your first crack at a new language, or just working on a website; it's free time for you to spend doing something constructive, in a group setting. We also have a lot of new development equipment available for you to play around with if you like, such as an Oculus Rift and a Raspberry Pi, so come out and join in!
